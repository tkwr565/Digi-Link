# DigiMap

> Find other Digimon fans in the real world.

Remember bumping into a stranger who also had a Digivice? DigiMap brings that back — for adults, anywhere on the planet.

---

## What it does

Drop a timed pin on the map when you're out and about with your Digivice. Other fans nearby can see your pin, check out your profile, and send you a message. Meet up, battle, and log it — no win/loss tracking, just the count.

---

## Features

- **Live map** — dark Digital World-themed map showing active fan pins near you
- **Location pins** — set a time window for when you'll be around (pins auto-expire)
- **Profiles** — pick your favourite Digimon as your avatar, list the Digivices you carry
- **Direct messages** — chat with fans who spotted your pin, tied to the pin that started the conversation
- **Battle log** — both players confirm a battle inside the DM thread; your total count goes up
- **Friends** — add fans you've met, get notified when friends drop new pins
- **Leaderboard** — see who's battled the most in the community

---

## How to use it

1. **Register** with your email (or Google)
2. **Set up your profile** — choose your favourite Digimon and add the Digivices you own
3. **Drop a pin** on the map when you're out — set how long you'll be available
4. **Tap any pin** on the map to see that fan's profile and send them a message
5. **Confirm a battle** inside the DM thread once you've met in real life — both players tap the button
